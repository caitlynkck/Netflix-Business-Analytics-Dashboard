# Netflix-Business-Analytics-Dashboard
Overview:
This project analyzes Netflix’s global content library to uncover insights into content composition, audience targeting, genre popularity, ratings, duration, and global production trends. The analysis is presented through an interactive Tableau dashboard designed for business and strategic decision-making.

Business Question: How does Netflix’s content strategy vary by content type, audience category, genre, and geography, and what trends drive recent platform growth?

Key Insights:
- Movies make up the majority of Netflix’s content catalog.

- Mature-rated content dominates the platform across most regions.

- A large share of titles were released within the last five years, indicating a focus on newer content.

- Content production is highly concentrated in a small number of countries, led by the U.S.

- Audience distribution and genre preferences vary by country.

Tools:
Tableau for dashboard development and visualization.
SQL for data cleaning and aggregating.
Excel and Google Sheets for preprocessing validation.

The analysis is organized into three interactive dashboards:
1. Content Overview – Movies vs. TV shows, genre distribution, and audience composition

2. Content Characteristics – Ratings, duration, seasons, and genre breakdowns

3. Global & Temporal Trends – Production by country, content growth over time, and release trends
